🔒 Privacy & Your Data
Your data stays on your device

All of your favorites, plans, and children's names are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on any server. Your data never leaves your device.

No accounts, no tracking

This site does not require an account, does not use cookies for tracking, and does not collect analytics about your usage. There is no login, no sign-up, and no way for us to see what you've saved.

Data does not sync across devices

Because your data is stored only in your browser, it will not appear if you visit this site on a different device, browser, or in a private/incognito window. If you clear your browser data, your favorites and plans will be erased.

Sharing your plan

If you choose to share your plan via email, the share feature opens your own email app with a pre-filled message. We never see or handle the email — it goes directly from your device to the recipient you choose. Please only share your children's schedule information with people you trust.

Camp information disclaimer

Camp details are gathered from publicly available sources and are updated periodically. We are not affiliated with any camp provider. Always verify dates, costs, and availability directly with each camp before enrolling your child.
